Actor

Of Polish descent, Philippe was born in France and grew up in Switzerland. After graduating from the National School of Dramatic Art of Saint-Étienne (France), he worked with Ludovic Lagarde, Séverine Chavrier, Yves-Noël Genod, Hervé Loichemol, Joan Mompart and Ivan Vyrypaev.

Biography

Philippe Tłokiński is an actor working in film, television and theatre. His work spans international productions.
